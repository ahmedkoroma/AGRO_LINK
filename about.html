ABOUT AGROLINK

Our Mission

To empower farmers in Sierra Leone with digital tools, smart planting guidance, and a marketplace that connects them directly to buyers.

Our Vision

To build a transparent, AI-driven agricultural ecosystem where farmers reduce crop losses, improve productivity, and access wider markets.

Why We Exist

Many farmers face losses due to poor soil, lack of real-time guidance, and difficulty reaching buyers. AGROLINK solves these issues by merging technology with agriculture in one simple platform.
